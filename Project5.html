Archimedes Trammel

November 2024 - December 2024

❮ ❯

This is a mechanically powered Archimedes Trammel or Do-Nothing machine for my dynamics class. This is basically a link attached to two othogonal sliders and is rotated at its center. This results in motion where the link is rotating at its center and the intersection of the sliders. The trammel has magnets on it so that a figure can magnetically attached and move with it. The paths of the motion (specifically the four corners of the figure) were analyzed using MotionGenesis at different points of the figure.
