Heatset Insert Press

March 2025 - March 2025

This was a quick and fun project I decided to do as I felt it would be useful for ASME section at SJSU. It is a press where a soldering iron can be attached to and insert heatset inserts in 3D printed material. The soldering iron holder is attached to a cutdown 2040 aluminum extrusion (I did not have 2020 on me at the time lol) using nylon wheels I 3D printed. The iron is balanced with a three compartment holder for heatset inserts. The press is attached to extrusions where components can locked down using the slots.
