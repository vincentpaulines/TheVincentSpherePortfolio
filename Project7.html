RC Airboat

January 2026 - Present

This is my newest project currently. It is an RC Airboat that will use contra-rotating propellers to move and be big enough for a plushie to ride on. Two rudders will be controlled by a servo attached to a link to make a four bar linakge. There will also be a bluetooth speaker onboard using a bluetooth module and selfmade audio amplifier. The entire boat will be 3D printed and made water tight using body filler.
